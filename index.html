Case Study:

DevRel Enablement Program for Specific EPAM Locations, Accounts, Major Projects, and Clients

DevRel Enablement Program empowers engineers, revolutionizing their professional growth and enhancing the overall Developer Experience. Through collaboration between Companyâ€™s organizational processes and developers, we aim to streamline talent engagement processes.

Challenges

Our program addresses the following challenges faced by engineers:

Tough People Integration

 

This particular challenge pertains to the complex task of integrating different employee groups with diverse corporate cultures and backgrounds, especially in the context of massive relocations, M&A cases, or mixed EPAM-Client teams environment. Navigating such diversity and ensuring a harmonious integration process is crucial for the overall success of the program, impacting growth, hiring, and retention processes within the organization.

Lack of Motivation to Extra-Mile Activities

 

Engineers with diverse backgrounds may miss opportunities or lack motivation to participate in activities beyond their projects. Additionally, some engineers lack confidence in public speaking and participating in events.

Weak Professional Communities

 

Locations or technologies/domains may face challenges such as a lack of community culture, poor professional networking, incomplete integration of employees with diverse backgrounds and experiences, and more. This becomes particularly crucial in the current context of our rapid expansion into new locations.

EPAM Employee Journey

 

At EPAM, certain locations are encountering challenges related to optimizing various stages of the employee lifecycle, aiming to attract, adapt, nurture continuous growth, and retain top talent.

Solutions Proposals:

Please note that the following list is not detailed. Our team recognizes the dynamic nature of our work and remains flexible to tailor solutions that best fit the evolving needs of our community and organization.
People Integration
At the company level, People Integration is all about addressing concerns and fostering a smooth transition with supportive management. We seize opportunities by introducing new projects, career prospects, benefits, education, and project bonuses.
On an individual (people) level, it's about showcasing values and role-model behavior, clearing up doubts about different cultures, and connecting people through networks, communities, and events. This holistic approach ensures a seamless integration process, promoting personal and professional growth for everyone involved.
